## 📖 场景描述 (Scene Description)

峨眉山下一方莲池。盛夏。满池白莲盛开——有的完全绽放如白玉盘，有的含苞待放如毛笔头，有的已结成莲蓬（莲子饱满）。碧绿的荷叶如伞盖层层叠叠——叶面上滚动着晶莹的水珠（清晨的露水）。池水清澈见底——可见几尾红鲤在莲茎之间穿游。周芷若（约三十五岁）赤足站在池边浅水中——身着素白麻布衣（最简单最朴素的衣料——麻的质地可见），长发随意绾在脑后。她弯下腰——双手轻轻捧起池水洗面。水从她指缝流下——在晨光中如碎银。她的脸上有水珠——分不清是池水还是别的什么。但她在笑——嘴角微微上扬，眼睛弯成月牙。她身后是峨眉山的晨曦——金顶在远处闪光。莲池边有一座小茅亭——亭中有石桌石凳，桌上放着笔墨和一册书稿。白莲花在她周围——仿佛她也是其中一朵。

## 🖼️ 构图指南 (Composition Guide)

环绕式构图。周芷若在画面中央偏下，周围的莲叶和莲花以她为圆心呈弧形分布——形成自然的"花环"构图。她的弯腰动作形成柔和的曲线。池水波纹从她脚下向外扩散（同心圆）。峨眉山在远景上方——天地轴线将她、莲池、金顶连成一线。小茅亭在画面左侧——平衡构图。

## 🎨 通用风格指南 (Universal Style Guide)

### 核心美术风格 (Core Art Style)
- **Primary**: 金庸武侠水墨风 (Jin Yong Wuxia Ink Painting Aesthetic)
- **Figure Style**: 中国工笔人物 (Chinese Gongbi Figure Painting) — fine, precise brushwork for faces, hands, and clothing details
- **Background Style**: 中国写意山水 (Chinese Xieyi Landscape) — expressive, atmospheric ink washes for environments
- **Fusion Approach**: Gongbi precision in foreground subjects + Xieyi freedom in backgrounds = balanced classical Chinese aesthetic

### 时代考据 (Historical Accuracy)
- **Period**: 元末明初 (Late Yuan / Early Ming Dynasty, c. 1350-1400)
- **Architecture**: Yuan Dynasty official buildings (Dougong brackets, hip-and-gable roofs, colored glazed tiles), Mongolian yurts (gers), Song-style garden pavilions
- **Clothing**:
  - Mongolian aristocracy: White sable furs, gold ornaments, leather boots, rounded-collar robes (质孙服)
  - Han Chinese women: Cross-collar ruqun (襦裙), beizi (褙子) outer jackets, flowing sleeves
  - Martial artists: Tight-cuffed training clothes, cloth boots, minimal accessories
- **Weapons**: Historically accurate Chinese swords (jian 剑), sabers (dao 刀), spears (qiang 枪)

### 构图原则 (Composition Principles)
- **Rule of Thirds**: Primary subject at 1/3 or 2/3 position, not dead center
- **Negative Space** (留白): Intentional empty areas for visual breathing room — a core Chinese painting principle
- **Depth Layers**: Foreground (details/framing) → Middle ground (main action) → Background (atmosphere)
- **Leading Lines**: Natural elements (rivers, paths, architectural lines) guiding eye to focal point
- **Scale Reference**: Include recognizable elements (trees, buildings, figures) for size context

### 色彩体系 (Color System)
- **Warm Earth Base**: 赭石 (burnt sienna), 土黄 (yellow ochre), 熟褐 (burnt umber)
- **Jade Accents**: 石青 (azurite blue), 石绿 (malachite green) for landscapes
- **Dramatic Reds**: 朱砂 (cinnabar red), 胭脂 (rouge) for important moments
- **Ink Blacks**: 焦墨 (burnt ink), 浓墨 (dark ink), 淡墨 (light ink) — five gradations of black
- **Gold Highlights**: 泥金 (gold paint) for divine light, sacred objects, sun rays
- **Avoid**: Neon colors, modern synthetic palettes, pure RGB primaries

### 光影处理 (Lighting Treatment)
- Classical Chinese painting traditionally uses flat/diffuse lighting
- For this project: Blend traditional flat lighting with subtle directional light
- Strong directional light ONLY in dramatic scenes (fire, divine intervention)
- Prefer: Soft, diffuse illumination that reveals form without harsh shadows
- Light source: Usually from upper-left or upper-right, gentle falloff
